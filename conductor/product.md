# Gofishi

## Description
A premium Boat Rental Marketplace (gofishi.com) focusing on Airbnb-style UI/UX for specific locations (Dermaga) and detailed listings for boats (Perahu).

## Goals
- Provide easy booking flows for users.
- Connect vendors (boat owners/captains) directly with clients via WhatsApp or online payments.
- Ensure all business validation logic, including a 17+ age check.
- Maintain stability of the backend as the highest priority before aesthetic changes.
